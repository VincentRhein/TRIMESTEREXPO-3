Kitchen-Oasis 2000

Noepy van Pelt

Using plastic drains & fittings, soil, plants and water, I have created a tool that is ‘going to change your life’! We no longer need to throw away perfectly usable water when washing the dishes. Instead we can use the water to WATER our plants, which we then use to feed ourselves and the air.
So, STOP wasting water! What are you waiting for?
